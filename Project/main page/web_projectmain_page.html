WHY WE CHOSE TO BUILD A GYM WEBSITE?
We saw the original Gold's gym website and after seeing it our team thought that we could build a better website with more functionality and beautiful design We divided our project into 8 pages (home , about us , profile, facilities,....) and each page has it own content and it's own functionality and also we add a page called profile (that is connected to database) for register new customer or an old customer wants to log in. We also added java-script to for our project to let the user have a better experience and make it easier while using the website for the design we decided to go with more photos and less words because we believe that a picture can worth a thousand words , so we went with more pictures of our coaches and our facilities and at the end of the page we added our social media accounts to make it easier to reach us, also we added our location at on of our pages through goolge maps. we also used responsive design in our project to make it easier for all users with different devices(Smartphones, computers, tablets,..etc)
